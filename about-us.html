Our Vision

At the core of the idea is the belief that real time genuine educational advices, if managed well, can make a positive difference to the student, to the parents, to the institutes, and to the society at a large scale. University Inspector is different things to different people-


For over a million students and parents struggling to get a meaningful perfect matched university every year, University Inspector is a guide, a mentor, and an affectionate friend.

For outsiders say; visitor, media, investors, friends and acquaintances; University Inspector is possibly the next big thing emerging as a platform for making the education system transparent.


But internally, we are a bunch of entrepreneurs trying to solve a problem of growing India.

Helping you get where you want to go

College is not one-size-fits-all. Everybody's different with their individual taste. We're here to help you find the right university that suits you by providing unbiased genuine reviews that helps you to get insight into the inside of your dream university before actually getting in, complete freedom from prevaricates college authorities.


Study hard, play hard

Are we talking about life at University? Nope. That's a normal day at University Inspector. Of course, we also bust our butts making the best tools to give you the most awesome experience possible. When you know how to play, who says you can't have it all?


Education seekers get a personalized experience on our site, based on educational background and career interest, enabling them to make well framed course and college decisions. The decision making is empowered with easy access to detailed information on career choices, courses, infrastructures, colleges, placement statistics, facilities provided, real time genuine reviews by interacting with current students in colleges and alumni groups.
